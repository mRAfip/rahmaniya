    Our History

    With over 40 years of excellence as RAC Boarding Madrasa, Rahmaniyya International Leading School, established in 2020-21, is committed to providing a well-rounded education in a morally enriched environment.

    At Rahmaniyya International Leading School, students are guided toward academic excellence while cultivating strong moral values and leadership qualities. By integrating religious and secular education, we prepare students with the knowledge and skills to excel in today’s world. Our focus on personal development, ethical grounding, and leadership ensures students are equipped to contribute positively to society and confidently face the challenges of a dynamic future.

    -Sayyid Sadiq Ali Shihab Thangal

    Rahmaniyya

    Special Facilities

    Local facilities for schooling refer to the essential infrastructure and resources within a community that support education.

    • Regular Schooling

      The institution provides higher secondary education in Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams.

    • Tuition Support and Coaching

      Tuition, coaching, and academic support play a crucial role in helping students strengthen their understanding of subjects, improve performance, and build confidence.

    • Competitive exam training

      Students receive guidance for medical and engineering entrance tests like NEET, JEE, and KEAM, ensuring they are well-prepared for higher education.

    • Madrasa Education

      Comprehensive study of the Quran, Hadith, Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence), and other essential Islamic subjects.
