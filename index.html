A Message from the Chairman

Welcome to Rahmania

With over 40 years of excellence as RAC Boarding Madrasa, Rahmaniyya International Leading School, established in 2020-21, is committed to providing a well-rounded education in a morally enriched environment.

At Rahmaniyya International Leading School, students are guided toward academic excellence while cultivating strong moral values and leadership qualities. By integrating religious and secular education, we prepare students with the knowledge and skills to excel in today’s world. Our focus on personal development, ethical grounding, and leadership ensures students are equipped to contribute positively to society and confidently face the challenges of a dynamic future.

-Sayyid Sadiq Ali Shihab Thangal
